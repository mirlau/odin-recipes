Sweet Potato Macaroni Cheese

Description

This sweet potato Mac and cheese is a variation of the classic Mac and cheese dish where sweet potato puree is added to the cheese sauce.

The sweet potato adds a hint of sweetness and a creamy texture to the sauce, making it a healthier and more nutritious version of the classic comfort food.

The dish is typically made by boiling and mashing sweet potatoes and adding them to a cheese sauce made with milk, butter, and shredded cheese.

The mixture is then combined with cooked pasta and baked until golden brown and bubbly. It's a delicious and satisfying dish that's perfect for a cozy dinner or a family gathering.
